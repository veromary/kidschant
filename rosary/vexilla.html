Vexilla Regis

The Royal Banners Forward Go

This tune has been set to some children's verse on page 17 and 18 of The Story of Redemption for Children

Vexilla Regis is a hymn once sung on Good Friday as the Blessed Sacrament was carried from the repository to the altar. It is also a Vespers hymn for Passiontide and the feast of the Holy Cross.

It was written by Venantius Fortunatus (530-609) in honor of the arrival of a relic of the True Cross sent to Queen Radegunda by the Emperor Justin II and his Empress Sophia. More information...
